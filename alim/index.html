Lessons You Should Learn
11/21/2017

This cohesive 8 track project, takes the time to illuminate key components that are required in any relationship. With Hit singles like Lost & Found and All Day, LiMM uses a great mix of melodies and rap to help illustrate exactly what characteristics are crucial in a relationship. A more confident and assertive sound is present in this Extended Play record, and it aids the artist in getting his point across clearly and smoothly.

The Effect [demo]
(date)

Limm’s The Effect [demo] is a project that metaphorically pens the first chapter in a successful music career. Songs such as Driving at Night and Been Thru Things are able to hone an R&B tone that separates Limm from the pack of upcoming artists and cements him as a name to remember for years to come. Songs off of the EP have streamed over 50K listens on SoundCloud and are still climbing. HipHopCanada describes The Effect [demo] as “A feel-good style of rap that feels as though we’re listening to the inner thoughts of the emcee.” and NxG describes it as “A six track journey saturated with emotional revelations and addictive melodies serving to validate Limm as a talented and upcoming artist.” When asked about it, Limm explains that throughout the project, “Each song grows on you until you are totally immersed and feeling the effect.”
